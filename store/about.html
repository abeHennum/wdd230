Our Story

Founded by a team of music lovers and vinyl aficionados, Vinyl Fountain was born out of a desire to revive the magic of vinyl records in a digital age. Our journey began with a shared vision: to create a haven where timeless classics and hidden gems can be rediscovered and appreciated anew. What started as a modest collection of cherished records has evolved into a vibrant community dedicated to preserving and sharing the joy of vinyl.

Our Mission

At Vinyl Fountain, our mission is simple: to provide an unparalleled selection of vinyl records that cater to every taste and genre. Whether you're a seasoned collector, a casual listener, or someone just beginning their vinyl journey, we strive to offer a curated assortment of records that are as diverse and unique as our customers. We are committed to sourcing vinyl records of the highest quality, ensuring that each one is carefully inspected and preserved to deliver optimal sound and enjoyment. From rare finds and limited editions to classic albums and new releases, our collection is designed to inspire and delight.

Salt Lake

Nestled in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, Salt Lake City is a vibrant and dynamic city that offers an exceptional blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and urban excitement. Known for its stunning mountain vistas and thriving arts scene, Salt Lake City is a place where every day holds the promise of adventure and discovery.
