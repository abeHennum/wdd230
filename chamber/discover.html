Daily Tea Consumption

Tea is a staple beverage in The Shire, with residents enjoying an average of 5 cups per day. This fondness for tea fuels a thriving tea trade, with specialty blends sourced from the finest tea gardens in Middle-earth.

Sheep Population

Sheep farming is a common occupation in The Shire, and it's estimated that there are 100 sheep for every Hobbit. The wool produced by these sheep is highly prized for its softness and quality, supporting a robust textile industry.

Events

Great Green Dragon Race

Strap on your running shoes and get ready for the adrenaline-pumping excitement of the Great Green Dragon Race! Compete against fellow racers in a thrilling dash across the Shire's lush countryside, navigating obstacles and overcoming challenges along the way. Cheer on the competitors as they vie for glory and bragging rights, and join in the post-race festivities with a lively awards ceremony and victory feast.

Shire Shindig

Let loose and dance the night away at the Shire Shindig, the biggest party of the year! Join fellow Shire-folk for an evening of music, merriment, and camaraderie under the twinkling stars. Groove to live performances by local bands playing toe-tapping tunes guaranteed to get you on your feet. Indulge in mouthwatering delicacies from food vendors lining the festival grounds, and make memories to last a lifetime with friends old and new.

Attractions

Brandywine Bridge

Cross the majestic Brandywine Bridge spanning the wide waters of the Brandywine River, a vital thoroughfare connecting the Eastfarthing and the Westfarthing of The Shire. Enjoy panoramic views of the tranquil countryside as you traverse this historic bridge, which has stood for centuries as a symbol of unity and friendship among the Hobbits.

Tookbank Vineyards

Indulge your senses with a visit to Tookbank Vineyards, The Shire's premier winery located in the heart of the Southfarthing. Take a guided tour of the vineyards and winemaking facilities to learn about the art of viticulture and sample a variety of award-winning wines crafted from locally grown grapes. Savor the flavors of the Shire terroir and toast to the good life amidst scenic vineyard vistas.
