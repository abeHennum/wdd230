BENEFITS

Non Profit

This membership type is for any business that is non profit and will only include a listing on the shires chamber of commerce

Bronze

Basic Recognition: Members receive acknowledgment as supporters of the Chamber.

Listing: Their business name and contact information are listed on the Chamber's website.

Networking Opportunities: Access to Chamber events and networking functions.

Visibility: Increased visibility within the local business community through Chamber communications and events.

Silver

Enhanced Visibility: Higher placement and more prominent recognition on the Chamber's website.

Marketing Opportunities: Opportunity to include promotional materials or special offers in Chamber communications.

Events Access: Priority access to certain Chamber events or discounted attendance fees.

Recognition: Silver members may receive a certificate or badge for display.

Gold

Top-tier Recognition: Highest level of visibility and recognition as a leading supporter of the Chamber.

Exclusive Benefits: Access to exclusive events or meetings with influential business leaders.

Premium Marketing: Highlighted in Chamber newsletters, social media, and press releases.

Leadership Opportunities: Invitation to participate in Chamber committees or advisory groups.

Special Awards: Potential for special awards or honors at Chamber events.
