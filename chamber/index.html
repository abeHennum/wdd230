Events

Hobbit Day Celebration

Join us in celebrating the birthdays of Bilbo and Frodo Baggins with a grand festival filled with feasts, music, dancing, and storytelling. Hobbit Day is a joyous occasion for all residents of the Shire and visitors alike. Come immerse yourself in the festive atmosphere and experience the magic of Middle-earth firsthand

Green Dragon Inn Beer Festival

Raise a pint and toast to good cheer at the annual Green Dragon Inn Beer Festival! This beloved event showcases the finest ales and brews from across the Shire, featuring a diverse selection of beers to tantalize your taste buds. Enjoy live music, friendly competitions, and the warm hospitality of the Green Dragon Inn. Whether you're a seasoned ale aficionado or simply looking for a good time, this festival promises fun for all.

Bywater Bakery

Bywater Bakery is a charming local bakery known for its mouthwatering pastries, fresh bread, and delectable pies. From flaky croissants to indulgent cakes, each treat is lovingly crafted using the finest ingredients and time-honored recipes. Stop by Bywater Bakery and treat yourself to a taste of sweetness.

Green Dragon Inn

The Green Dragon Inn is a beloved establishment in Hobbiton, offering hearty meals, refreshing beverages, and lively entertainment. Whether you're looking to enjoy a pint with friends, savor a delicious meal, or simply soak up the cozy atmosphere, The Green Dragon Inn welcomes you with open arms.

Took's Toy Shop

Took's Toy Shop is a whimsical emporium filled with handcrafted toys, games, and novelties. From wooden puzzles to plush toys, each item is lovingly made by the creative minds of the Took family. Whether you're shopping for a special occasion or simply looking to brighten someone's day, Took's Toy Shop has something for everyone.
